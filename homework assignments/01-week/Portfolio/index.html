About Me

Hello there, my name is John Petty. I am originally from Provo Utah. I graduated from Provo High School in 1990. In 1992-1993, I served a full-time mission for the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter Day Saints.

Shortly thereafter, I started working on my Bachelor of Science in Electrical Engineering Technology at Brigham Young University.

After graduating from BYU in 1999, I started working for Novell Inc. as a Software Test Engineer for the OS team. Two years later, I moved within the company to work as a Technical Support Engineer for the Asia Pacific Region taking support calls speaking Cantonese.
